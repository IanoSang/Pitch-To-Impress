The Elevator Pitch

An elevator pitch is a type of speech about your company or brand. No more than 30 seconds in length (it should be short enough to cover in an elevator ride, hence its name), itâ€™s a type of sales pitch that every sales professional should have up their sleeve.

Sales Pitch

A sales pitch is a well-crafted, then packaged sales presentation. A good sales pitch needs to convey the intended message concisely in a compelling manner. If the sales pitch is on point, you are on the right path to making profitable sales.

product pitch

A product pitch is not much different than a sales pitch, but is specifically focused on a product or service. You'll go in-depth and emphasize how your product works, how it will solve their pain points, and the specific benefits it will bring to your customers.
